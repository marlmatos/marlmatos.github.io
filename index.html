Albert Einstein College of Medicine

I am a PhD student in the Genetics Department at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine and visiting student at the New York Genome Center, working with John Greally and Tuuli Lappalainen. I am interested functional genetic variation and mechanisms affecting human traits and disease. My current research focuses on characterizing inter-individual differences on cellular and molecular phenotypes within the T Cell compartment during healthy aging.
